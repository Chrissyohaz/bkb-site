When to choose a Gear service

Servicing your gears is a bit like a car service, if it is done regularly it will reduce wear and tear and ensure that your bike runs smoothly for longer. The staff in our stores will be able to advise you in detail, but in general we would recommend you choose a Gear Service:

If the gears on your bike are not working properly

If the chain is slipping, rusty or stiff

After winter to ensure that your bike is restored and ready for spring

At least once per year to reduce wear and tear and improve lifespan

When to choose a Brake service

Our Brake Service is great value for a reason – we think that getting your brakes checked regularly is really important to keep your bike safe. The staff in our stores will be able to advise you in detail, but in general we would recommend you choose a Brake Service:

If your brakes lack stopping power

If your brakes feel spongy, rough or soft

If your brakes are making excessive noise

At least once per year to ensure they are safe

When to choose a Wheel service

As the main contact point with the road or trail, the wheels really take the brunt of the terrain. Be it potholes, roots or just everyday use, it is common for wheels to lose spoke tension, snap spokes or for hubs to stop spinning smoothly. The staff in the store will be able to advise you in detail, but we would generally recommend you choose a Wheel Service:

If your spokes feel loose, you have some broken spokes, or your brakes are rubbing intermittently

If your hubs feel rough or there is play in the wheel itself

After winter to ensure that your bike is restored and ready for spring

At least every 12-18 Months
